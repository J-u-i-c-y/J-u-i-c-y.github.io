St Paul’s Juniors

St Paul's Juniors is the junior division for St Paul's School. Educating and engaging boys aged 7-13 years, who are curious about life and have a hunger for learning.

Admissions

Boys can join at 7+, 8+ or 11+. The closing date for registration for 7+ and 8+ entry in September 2020 is Friday 4 October 2019.
